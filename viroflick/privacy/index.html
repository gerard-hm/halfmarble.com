ViroFlick — Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 6, 2026

ViroFlick is a game published by Halfmarble LLC (“halfmarble”, “we”, “us”). This policy explains how ViroFlick handles your information.

The short version: ViroFlick does not collect any data about you. There are no accounts, no sign-in, no analytics, no advertising, and no third-party tracking.

Data we collect

None. ViroFlick does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data, and it does not track you across other apps or websites. There is no advertising identifier (IDFA) and no attribution.

Information stored on your device

Your game settings, progress, evolutions, and best scores are stored locally on your device by iOS. This information never leaves your device through ViroFlick, and it is removed when you delete the app.

Game Center

If you choose to use Game Center, your leaderboard scores are submitted to Apple’s Game Center service and handled by Apple under Apple’s Privacy Policy. ViroFlick does not collect, store, or receive this information itself, and using Game Center is optional. To keep scores private, you can turn Game Center off in your device Settings.

Purchases

ViroFlick offers one optional in-app purchase. Purchases are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store; ViroFlick never receives your payment information.

Third parties

ViroFlick contains no third-party SDKs — no analytics, advertising, crash-reporting, or social frameworks. No third-party partners receive any data from the app.

Children

ViroFlick does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children. Because we collect no data at all, no personal information from children (or adults) is gathered.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a new effective date.
