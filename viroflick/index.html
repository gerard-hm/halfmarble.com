The // counter cycle

Each cell destroys exactly one pathogen type. Fire the counter for a clean kill — a wrong type only chips, and a pathogen's own type heals it.

What's // inside

●Charge & overcharge

Hold an orb to charge a cell — it hits harder and pierces, carrying leftover punch to the next pathogen. Hold too long and it overcharges and locks. Release in the window.

●Nine pathogen kinds

Rushers, dividing cells that split on death, shielded shells, worm segments, and a satellite-ringed strain that morphs element every time you hurt it.

●Medical Textbook

A built-in reference for every cell, pathogen, case, and tactic — with live mechanism loops, so you can study the counter cycle before you're under fire.

●Game Center leaderboards

Optional ranked scores, kept per case combination. Leave Game Center off in Settings and your scores stay entirely private.

●No accounts, no ads, no tracking

ViroFlick collects no data about you — no sign-in, no analytics, no advertising, no third-party SDKs. Settings and scores live on your device.

●One honest unlock

Free to download with two full cases. A single one-time purchase opens all six — tied to your Apple Account, no subscriptions, no consumables.

Private by design

There are no accounts and no cloud sync. Your settings, evolutions, and best scores are stored on your device only — the same local-first stance behind everything halfmarble builds.
