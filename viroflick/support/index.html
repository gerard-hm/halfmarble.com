ViroFlick — Support

For ViroFlick on iPhone and iPad

ViroFlick is a biology-themed tower-defense game by Halfmarble LLC (“halfmarble”). Flick swarms of cells at invading pathogens and keep your patient’s vitals up.

Need help? Email gerard@halfmarble.com. Include your device model, iOS version, and a short description of what happened.

How do I play?

Swirl a vial with your finger to charge it, then flick to launch a swarm of cells at a pathogen. Each cell type is strong against the next one in the cycle: Antibody beats Toxin, Toxin beats Enzyme, Enzyme beats Oxygen, and Oxygen beats Antibody. Match the right vial to each invader. The in-game Medical Textbook explains every case and the counter cycle.

My purchase isn’t showing up

ViroFlick has one optional purchase: a one-time unlock that opens all six cases. It is tied to your Apple Account, so it carries across your devices and reinstalls. If it doesn’t show up:

  1. Make sure you are signed in with the same Apple Account you bought it with.
  2. Tap RESTORE PURCHASES on the unlock screen in the app.

Payments and refunds are handled by Apple, not by us. To request a refund, visit reportaproblem.apple.com.

Where is my progress saved?

Your settings, evolutions, and best scores are stored on your device only. There are no accounts and no cloud sync — if you delete the app, your progress is deleted with it.

Game Center and leaderboards

Leaderboards use Apple’s Game Center and are optional. If your scores aren’t submitting, check that Game Center is turned on in your device Settings. To keep your scores private, turn Game Center off there.

What do I need to run it?

An iPhone or iPad running iOS 17 or later.

Privacy

ViroFlick collects no data about you — no accounts, no analytics, no ads, no tracking. Read the full privacy policy.
