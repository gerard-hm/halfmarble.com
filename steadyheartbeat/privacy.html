STEADYHEARTBEAT · iOS APP

Privacy Policy

SteadyHeartBeat is built on a simple principle: your health data belongs to you. It is processed entirely on your device, and halfmarble never receives it.

Last updated: June 11, 2026

01What data the app reads

All reads happen on-device via Apple HealthKit

SteadyHeartBeat reads health data through Apple's HealthKit framework. All processing happens on your iPhone. No data is transmitted to halfmarble or any third party.

02What data the app writes

Workout sessions are saved to your Health library (optional)

When you complete a monitoring session, SteadyHeartBeat saves a workout record to Apple Health by default — including the session duration, activity type (boxing, cycling, running, walking, hiking, or other), heart rate, and active calories burned — so it counts toward your Apple Fitness rings. This data belongs to you, is governed by Apple's Health privacy settings, and then follows your own iCloud Health sync settings. You can turn this off in the app under Preferences → Apple Health → Save workouts to Apple Health; with it off, the workout is discarded and never written to Apple Health.

Completed session summaries — HR timeline, zone distribution, and histogram — and your on-device profile (age, biological sex, any self-reported conditions, and manually entered metrics) are saved to the app's private storage on your iPhone for the Sessions history and pre-workout screens. This storage is explicitly excluded from iCloud and iTunes backups, so it stays on your device.

03What we will never do

Hard commitments, not marketing language
✕ Sell your health data. Your HealthKit and Health Records data will never be sold to any third party, including data brokers, advertisers, or analytics firms.
✕ Use your data for advertising. HealthKit data is never used to serve targeted advertising within this app or on other platforms.
✕ Share with pharmaceutical companies without consent. We will never share data with pharmaceutical or biotech companies except under IRB-approved research agreements with published findings — and only with your explicit, informed, separately-granted consent. (This research opt-in does not yet exist; when it ships, it will be a standalone screen, not buried in settings.)
✕ Transmit raw health data to our servers. All computation — zone calculation, HR analysis, histogram generation — happens on your device. halfmarble does not operate a server that receives personal health data.
✕ Retain data if you delete the app. Deleting SteadyHeartBeat from your iPhone removes all locally stored session data. Workout records already saved to Apple Health remain in Health (which you control via the Health app).

04Export and delete, on demand

Locked down must not mean locked in

Keeping your data on-device must not lock you out of it. Preferences → Your Data gives you two unconditional actions — no account, no request form, no waiting:

Export My Data assembles everything the app stores — your workout sessions and on-device profile — into a complete, readable copy and hands it to the iOS share sheet. Where it goes is your choice; the app does not upload it anywhere. The export sheet also offers Anonymize for Research…, which produces a de-identified copy (random identifiers; identity and absolute timestamps removed) for you to donate to a research effort of your own choosing. halfmarble receives nothing in either case. (Where halfmarble itself runs opt-in research, it never takes individual records — only differential-privacy-protected aggregates; see our data policy.)

Delete All Data immediately erases every session and the on-device profile. Workout records you already saved to Apple Health remain in Health, under your control there.

05Website analytics

This website only — not the app

This website (halfmarble.com) uses Plausible Analytics, a privacy-first analytics tool that collects no personal data, sets no cookies, and is fully GDPR-compliant. Aggregate page view counts are the only data collected. The SteadyHeartBeat iOS app does not include any analytics SDK.

06Children

Not directed at children under 13

SteadyHeartBeat is not directed at children under 13 years of age and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
