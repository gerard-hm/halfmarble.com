STEADYHEARTBEAT · iOS APP

Privacy Policy

SteadyHeartBeat is built on a simple principle: your health data belongs to you. It never leaves your device.

Last updated: June 1, 2026

01What data the app reads

All reads happen on-device via Apple HealthKit

SteadyHeartBeat reads health data through Apple's HealthKit framework. All processing happens on your iPhone. No data is transmitted to halfmarble or any third party.

02What data the app writes

Workout sessions are saved to your Health library

When you complete a monitoring session, SteadyHeartBeat saves a workout record to Apple Health. This record includes the session duration, activity type (boxing, cycling, running, walking, hiking, or other), heart rate samples, and active calories burned. This data belongs to you and is governed by Apple's Health privacy settings.

Completed session summaries — including HR timeline, zone distribution, and histogram — are also saved to the app's private storage on your iPhone for display in the Sessions history screen. This data is included in your iCloud backup if iCloud backup is enabled on your device.

03What we will never do

Hard commitments, not marketing language
✕ Sell your health data. Your HealthKit and Health Records data will never be sold to any third party, including data brokers, advertisers, or analytics firms.
✕ Use your data for advertising. HealthKit data is never used to serve targeted advertising within this app or on other platforms.
✕ Share with pharmaceutical companies without consent. We will never share data with pharmaceutical or biotech companies except under IRB-approved research agreements with published findings — and only with your explicit, informed, separately-granted consent. (This research opt-in does not yet exist; when it ships, it will be a standalone screen, not buried in settings.)
✕ Transmit raw health data to our servers. All computation — zone calculation, HR analysis, histogram generation — happens on your device. halfmarble does not operate a server that receives personal health data.
✕ Retain data if you delete the app. Deleting SteadyHeartBeat from your iPhone removes all locally stored session data. Workout records already saved to Apple Health remain in Health (which you control via the Health app).

04Website analytics

This website only — not the app

This website (halfmarble.com) uses Plausible Analytics, a privacy-first analytics tool that collects no personal data, sets no cookies, and is fully GDPR-compliant. Aggregate page view counts are the only data collected. The SteadyHeartBeat iOS app does not include any analytics SDK.

05Children

Not directed at children under 13

SteadyHeartBeat is not directed at children under 13 years of age and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

06Changes to this policy

We'll tell you when the rules change

If we materially change how we handle your data, we will update this page and change the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the app after a material change constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
